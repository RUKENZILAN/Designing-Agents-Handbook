Meet the Author

Ruken Zilan is an AI researcher, educator, and physicist with over 20 years of experience spanning academia and industry. She holds degrees in Physics from Gazi University and METU, and an M.Sc. in Computer Engineering from TOBB ETU. A former CISCO CA research fellow, she carried out PhD-level studies at Universitat Politècnica de Catalunya (UPC Barcelona Tech) and the Barcelona Supercomputing Center (BSC). Throughout her career, Ruken has driven R&D across major technoparks including METU Technopolis, ITU ARI Technopark, and Istanbul Technopark. Currently, she shapes the future of AI education by designing and teaching university-level Prompt Engineering curricula while delivering industry-wide workshops.
